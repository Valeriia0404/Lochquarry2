Welcome to Lochquarry

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills. On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery, but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities. With activities designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people, Lochquarry truly brings adventure to everyone. Lochquarry Outdoor Centre offers a wide range of exciting activities for young people aged 6 to 18.

  • Land activities
  • Water activities
  • Rope activities

Whether you enjoy land, water or rope-based activities, there is something for everyone!
