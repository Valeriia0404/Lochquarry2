About Lochquarry

Located in the Argyll hills, Lochquarry Outdoor Centre offers safe and exciting activities for all experience levels.

Each activity is run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided. Let’s meet some of the Lochquarry staff:

  • Claire Jack - Manager

    • Responsible for: The overall running of the centre and all of its activities

    Favourite Activity: Pole climb
  • Robbie Elliot - Instructor

    • Responsible for: Overseeing all of the land based activities Favourite Activity: Hillwalking in the beautiful Scottish highlands

  • Marion Hunter - Administrator

    • Responsible for: Making bookings and arranging activity slots for groups Favourite Activity: Making sure everyone has a great time when the visit Lochquarry

All activities are led by trained staff and include full safety equipment.
